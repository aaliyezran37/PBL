About game

In this game, you go for an adventure in Skywards City, where you save the city from the terrors of the Xanthrians. Get enough coins in each level, and complete the levels on time!

Storyline

In 1456, a thriving territory named Skywards City was invaded by the Xanthrian Empire. The Xanthrians' tyrant leader, Jámes Rodrigo del Xanthros, enforces the people of Skywards to pay a small tax. Four years later, most of the Skyvarians have either been arrested, executed or enslaved by the Xanthrians. David Shelbeck, a wealthy person is fed up with the Xanthrians. After the execution of his close friend, William Sheringham, he took the noble decision to fight the Xanthrians.
